How the application works

1. Query zodiac

Choose your birth date in date box.
Then the application will display your zodiac below the input box.
You can re-enter birth date without limit.

2. View zodiac detail

Use your mouse to hover on the zodiac sign and it is highlighted.
You can click the mouse to select the zodiac sign.
Then the detail of this zodiac is shown below the signs.
Note that when your mouse leaves the sign, the message will disappear.
